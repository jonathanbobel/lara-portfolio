2015 Dealer Tire Annual Report

This was the last annual report I designed that didn't have to follow a Dealer Tire brand – since it didn’t exist yet. Working closely with my copywriter and photographer I was able to highlight each department with a colorful photo representing them. Each department included the Partner, a quote, an overview, challenges, accomplishments, and main objective.

Depending on key accomplishments, each department had the ability to include metrics specific to their goals. Keeping a similar flow helped each department supply quality information that we were able to mold into a progressive story for the year.
