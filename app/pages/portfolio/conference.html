2019 Jaguar/Land Rover Dealer Conference

Create a B2B and B2C booth for the Jaguar/Land Rover Annual Conference with a tire centric message that was clear and concise. Both events took place in Coachella, CA and the creative direction I took utilized the latitude/longitude coordinates. I initiated design layouts for the B2C booth while waiting for approval on the B2B booth. I collaborated with the copywriter and stakeholders to make sure the messaging and design complimented each other and worked within JLRs brand.

The B2B booth was successful due to the number of visitors and email signups. Because of my initiative with the B2C booth we gained approval and Goodyear agreed to partner with us giving way to the funding needed.
